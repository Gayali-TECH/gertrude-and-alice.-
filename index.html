ABOUT

Gertrude & Alice Bookstore is a much-loved Bondi institution with a rich literary history that spans almost twenty years. An oasis for readers, writers, thespians and coffee-lovers alike, it’s a place where lovers of all things literary can seek solace between the pages of a good book. 

Come and enjoy an early morning coffee, our famous chai tea, or one of our delicious, home-made sweet treats, and browse our endless offering of both new and second-hand books for your next best read.

We Accept Used Books!

We accept donations of used books. We make sure nothing ends up in landfill by distributing books between our shop, our free boxes, public libraries, and charities such as Lifeline. 

Rare Finds for Collectors!

Our collection includes unique and rare books that are perfect for collectors or anyone seeking a one-of-a-kind treasure. Let us help you uncover your next gem.

Hosting Events and Book Clubs!

From author signings and book launches to regular book club meetings, we bring book lovers together to share their passion for reading. Check out our calendar for upcoming events!
